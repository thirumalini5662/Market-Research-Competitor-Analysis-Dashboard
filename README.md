# Market-Research-Competitor-Analysis-Dashboard
A Market Research & Competitor Analysis project focused on the Indian Food Delivery Industry. The project uses Excel, Power BI, competitor benchmarking, SWOT analysis, customer sentiment analysis, and market trend evaluation to identify competitive positioning and industry opportunities.

Project Overview

This project analyzes the competitive landscape of India's food delivery industry using market research and business intelligence techniques. The objective is to evaluate competitors, identify strengths and weaknesses, assess market trends, and generate strategic recommendations through interactive visualizations.

Objectives

- Analyze competitor performance and market positioning
- Compare key features using a benchmarking matrix
- Evaluate customer sentiment and market perception
- Study industry growth trends and user adoption
- Generate business insights and recommendations

Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- SWOT Analysis
- Competitor Benchmarking
- Customer Sentiment Analysis
- Market Trend Analysis

Key Metrics

- Market Share (%)
- App Ratings
- Monthly Orders
- Delivery Fees
- Active Users
- Market Growth Rate
- CAGR

Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Competitor Market Share Analysis
- App Rating Comparison
- Delivery Fee Comparison
- Monthly Orders Analysis
- Market Growth Trend
- Active User Trend
- Competitor Benchmarking Heatmap

Business Insights

- Identified leading competitors based on market share and customer ratings.
- Evaluated competitive strengths and weaknesses through SWOT analysis.
- Analyzed customer sentiment to understand consumer preferences.
- Studied industry growth trends and future opportunities.
- Developed strategic recommendations for market expansion and customer retention.

Project Outcome

The project demonstrates practical business analysis skills including market research, competitive intelligence, dashboard development, data visualization, and strategic decision-making.
